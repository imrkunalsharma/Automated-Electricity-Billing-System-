Right to Information

Bijli Bill System

Overview

The Right to Information (RTI) empowers citizens to access information related to the Bijli Bill system, promoting transparency and accountability in the electricity sector.

Key Aspects

  • Access to Information: Request information about billing practices and tariffs.
  • Transparency: Understand how bills are calculated and any changes to tariffs.
  • Consumer Rights: Learn about your rights related to billing and complaints.
  • Documentation: Request documents such as billing guidelines and service contracts.
  • Timely Responses: Authorities must respond to requests within a specified period.
  • Exemptions: Some sensitive information may be exempt from disclosure.
  • Filing Process: Instructions on how to file RTI requests effectively.
