Bijli Bill System Policy

Overview

The Bijli Bill system governs the billing and payment process for electricity consumption.

Key Policies

  • Tariff Structure: Different rates apply based on usage levels.
  • Billing Cycle: Monthly or bi-monthly billing.
  • Payment Methods: Various options including online and in-person payments.
  • Late Fees: Charges may apply if payment is late.
  • Disconnection Policy: Service may be disconnected for non-payment.
  • Consumer Rights: Protection against unfair billing practices.
  • Subsidies and Relief Programs: Assistance for low-income households.
