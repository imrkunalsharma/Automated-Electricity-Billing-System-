Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Billing & Payment FAQs

How can I view my electricity bill online?

You can view your bill by logging into your account and clicking on the "View Bill" option. You will need your customer ID or account number.

What payment methods are available to pay my electricity bill?

You can pay your bill via net banking, debit/credit card, UPI, NEFT/RTGS, and mobile wallets.

Can I set up automatic bill payments?

Yes, you can set up auto-debit for your bill payments through net banking or a registered credit/debit card.

Is there a penalty for late bill payments?

Yes, a late payment surcharge may apply if your bill is not paid by the due date. The details will be mentioned in your bill.

Account Management FAQs

How can I register for an account on the portal?

You can register by clicking the "Register" button on the home page and providing your account number, mobile number, and email.

I forgot my login password. How can I reset it?

You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link and following the instructions to recover your account.

How do I update my contact details (mobile number/email)?

After logging into your account, go to the "Account Settings" section and update your contact information.

How can I link my Aadhaar/PAN to my electricity account?

You can update your Aadhaar or PAN details under the "Update KYC" section after logging into your account.

How do I check my electricity usage history?

You can view your past consumption history by selecting the "Billing History" or "Consumption History" option in your account.

Metering & Consumption FAQs

How is my electricity consumption calculated?

Your consumption is calculated based on the reading of your electricity meter, which records the total energy used over a billing cycle.

What is the process for reporting incorrect meter readings?

If you believe your meter reading is incorrect, you can file a complaint online or visit your local utility office. An official will recheck the meter.

How can I track my daily electricity usage?

If you have a smart meter, you can track your daily usage through the portal or mobile app. Non-smart meters can only provide monthly data.

How can I apply for a new electricity connection?

To apply for a new connection, visit the "New Connection" section on the portal and fill out the required details.

Support & Complaints FAQs

How do I lodge a complaint regarding my bill or service?

You can register a complaint through the "Register Complaint" option on the portal. Alternatively, you can call the customer care number provided on the website.

What should I do if my electricity connection is disconnected?

If your connection is disconnected due to non-payment, you can pay the pending dues online and apply for reconnection through the "Reconnection" section.

How can I check the status of my complaint?

You can track the status of your registered complaint in the "Check Status" section using your complaint reference number.

Who should I contact in case of a power outage?

You can report power outages through the portal or by contacting the nearest service center. Emergency helpline numbers are provided in the "Contact Us" section.
